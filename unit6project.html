Who is Peep?

Born Gustav Åhr in Ronkonkoma New York on November 1, 1966, Lil Peep began his rap career in 2015. It wasn't until his release of Crybaby and Hellboy in 2016 that Peep saw success in the music industry. Peep has been recognized by the New York Times as the "Kurt Cobain" of lo-fi rap. After starting off on YouTube and Soundcloud, Peep has now added his music to Spotify and Apple Music, both well known and widely used streaming services.
